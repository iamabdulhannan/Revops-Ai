Engagement Strategy

Phase 1: Warm Introduction (Week 1-2)

  • Send validation survey to VP of Sales / Head of Revenue Ops at each Tier 1 company
  • Leverage Pakistan tech community connections (Arbisoft, VentureDive, 10Pearls are well-connected)
  • Use LinkedIn InMail with personalized pain-point messaging per company
  • Offer free Revenue Health Audit as engagement hook

Phase 2: Demo & Validation (Week 3-4)

  • Schedule 30-min revenue health audit calls with interested leads
  • Show Services Revenue Mode features specifically built for their model
  • Present AI forecasting with mock data similar to their business
  • Collect feedback for product iteration before full launch

Phase 3: Pilot Program (Month 2-3)

  • Offer first 3 Tier 1 customers a 3-month pilot at 50% discount
  • Get case studies + testimonials from pilot customers
  • Use pilot feedback to refine Services Revenue Mode features
  • Expand to Tier 2 companies with proven case studies

Key Outreach Channels by Country

  • Pakistan: P@SHA events, PSEB directory, local tech meetups, LinkedIn (strong community)
  • India: NASSCOM events, SaaSBOOMi community, LinkedIn, G2/Capterra listings
  • Bangladesh: BASIS members directory, Dhaka tech meetups, Nordic tech community bridge events
  • Sri Lanka: SLASSCOM directory, Colombo tech community, CSE-listed company investor relations
